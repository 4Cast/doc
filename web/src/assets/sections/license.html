License

A license is required to enable some features on Wakanda Enterprise Server. You can get one on Wakanda.io.

How to use your license.key

There are three options describe below by priority order:

1. CLI option

You can specify your license when running your wakanda Server
  • Windows:
    > wakanda-server.exe -l path/to/license.key
    > wakanda-server.exe --license path/to/license.key
  • Mac:
    $ wakanda-server -l path/to/license.key
    $ wakanda-server --license path/to/license.key
  • Linux:
    $ wakanda-server -l path/to/license.key
    $ wakanda-server --license path/to/license.key

2. Solution option

Add your license in the solution folder.
SOLUTION/license/license.key

3. Document option

You can add your license in your document folder:
  • Windows: C:/Users/<user>/Documents/Wakanda/license/license.key
  • Mac: /Users/<user>/Documents/Wakanda/license/license.key
  • Linux: /home/<user>/.wakanda/UserDoc/Wakanda/license/license.key
Prefer the Document option if your are in development mode.
Prefer the Solution option if your are in production mode.
